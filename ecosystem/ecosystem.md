# Ecosystem

Nowadays, electricity is an essential part of life. Energy prices represent a significant portion of our domestic expenditures, determine industrial competitiveness, and influence energy consumption. With technology development, the electricity market is also changing every year.

> Many factors affect electricity demand and consumption, such as environmental, economic, and social factors.&#x20;

End-use prices paid by final consumers are affected by movements in commodity markets, as well as by policy decisions. According to seasons, demands, and many variables, electricity prices vary from country to country.

{% hint style="info" %}
The energy price increases as electricity consumption increases and fossil fuel consumption decreases.
{% endhint %}

For example, in the first half of 2021, compared with the same period in 2020, prices were 54% higher.
